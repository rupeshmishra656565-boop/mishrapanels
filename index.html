What We Do

Comprehensive Panel Solutions

From custom manufacturing to emergency repairs — full-spectrum electrical panel services for industrial and commercial facilities across Maharashtra.

01

Custom Manufacturing

We design and build bespoke PCC, MCC, and Blokset panels from scratch — tailored precisely to your power requirements, site conditions, and load specifications.

02

Expert Repair & Servicing

Rapid diagnostic and repair for all panel types. With 25 years in the field, we identify faults quickly and accurately to minimize your operational downtime and losses.

03

Busbar & Busduct Systems

Precision installation, maintenance, and repair of high-capacity Busbar and Busduct routing — ensuring safe, efficient power distribution throughout your facility.

04

Professional Installation

Flawless on-site installation and commissioning of all electrical panels, adhering to the highest IS/IEC safety standards, with full documentation handover.

05

Retrofitting & Upgrades

Upgrade aging switchboards and control panels with modern, energy-efficient components — without the full replacement cost. Future-proof your infrastructure today.

06

Preventative Maintenance

Scheduled testing, thermal imaging, cleaning, and certification to prevent unexpected failures, extend equipment life, and maintain IS compliance year-round.
